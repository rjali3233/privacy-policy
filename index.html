Privacy Policy for AK Music Player

Effective Date: April 3, 2026

AK Music Player ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

AK Music Player is a local offline music player. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal identifiable information (PII) to any external servers.

Required Permissions:

  • Storage / Media Files: Our app requires access to your device's internal and external storage to scan, index, and play audio files (MP3, WAV, etc.) stored locally on your device. This is a core functionality.
  • Bluetooth: We access Bluetooth settings to allow the app to connect and stream audio to your Bluetooth speakers or headphones for an enhanced listening experience.
  • Device State: We access device state to automatically pause music when you receive an incoming phone call.

2. Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any user data, we do not sell, trade, or share any information with third parties. All your music data remains strictly on your own device.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs that track user behavior.

4. Security

We value your trust. Since the app operates offline for its core features, your data is protected by the security measures of your Android operating system.
